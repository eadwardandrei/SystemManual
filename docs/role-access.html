Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

TechReserve implements a Role-Based Access Control model to ensure secure, organized, and permission-driven access to system features. Each user role is granted access only to functionalities relevant to their responsibilities.

Account Hierarchy

Administrator

Highest authority with full system control.

Employee / Staff

Operational role supporting reservations and deployments.

Borrower

End-user role for requesting and tracking reservations.

ROLE DEFINITIONS

Role Capabilities & Permissions

Administrator

Full system authority responsible for governance, security, and configuration of TechReserve.

Available Permissions

  • Create, update, activate, or deactivate all user accounts
  • Assign and modify user roles and access levels
  • View and manage all reservations and deployment records
  • Configure system modules, rules, and policies
  • Generate comprehensive system and audit reports

Conditional Permissions

  • Delete user accounts (requires system confirmation)
  • Override reservation conflicts when policy conditions are met
  • Modify historical records with audit logging enabled

Employee / Staff

Operational role assisting administrators in reservation processing and resource management.

Available Permissions

  • View reservation requests assigned to their department
  • Update equipment deployment and return status
  • Manage availability of assigned equipment and venues
  • View operational and task-related reports
  • Edit personal account information

Conditional Permissions

  • Approve or reject reservations (if delegated by admin)
  • Flag damaged or missing equipment for review
  • Modify reservation schedules within assigned scope

Borrower

End-user role responsible for requesting, tracking, and utilizing institutional resources.

Available Permissions

  • Browse available equipment, facilities, and schedules
  • Submit new reservation requests
  • Track reservation approval and deployment status
  • View personal reservation history
  • Update personal profile and credentials

Conditional Permissions

  • Cancel reservations before approval or deployment
  • Extend borrowing period upon admin approval
  • Submit multiple requests subject to system limits
