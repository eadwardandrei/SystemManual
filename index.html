About TechReserve

TechReserve is an institutional Equipment and Venue Reservation System developed to support FEU Tech’s operational and academic activities. It centralizes the request, approval, and management of shared resources to ensure efficiency, transparency, and accountability across departments.

Purpose of the System

The primary goal of TechReserve is to streamline reservation workflows by replacing manual and fragmented processes with a single, organized digital platform. This reduces delays, prevents scheduling conflicts, and improves resource utilization.

Who the System Is For

TechReserve is designed for students, faculty members, and institutional staff who require access to equipment and venues, as well as administrators who manage approvals, assignments, and system operations.

Key Design Principles

  • User-friendly and guided workflows
  • Role-based access and security
  • Clear approval and tracking mechanisms
  • Reliable reporting and auditability

System Scope

The system covers reservation submission, approval workflows, resource management, personnel assignment, and report generation. It is intended for internal institutional use and aligns with FEU Tech’s operational policies.

Account Hierarchy

The system follows a role-based hierarchy consisting of Users, Administrators, and System Managers, each with clearly defined access levels and permissions.
