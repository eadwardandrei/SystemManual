About TechReserve

TechReserve is FEU Tech’s centralized Equipment and Venue Reservation System, built to bring structure, accountability, and clarity to how shared institutional resources are requested, approved, and managed.

System Purpose

The system replaces manual, fragmented reservation processes with a single digital platform—reducing delays, eliminating conflicts, and ensuring that institutional resources are utilized efficiently and fairly.

Who It Serves

TechReserve supports users who request equipment and venues for academic and operational needs, while empowering administrators to oversee approvals, assignments, and system governance with confidence.

Guiding Principles

  • Clear and guided user workflows
  • Role-based access and accountability
  • Transparent approval and tracking mechanisms
  • Reliable reporting and audit readiness

System Scope

The system covers reservation submission, approval workflows, resource and personnel management, notification handling, and administrative reporting—aligned with FEU Tech’s institutional policies and internal operations.

Account Hierarchy

The system follows a role-based hierarchy consisting of Users, Administrators, and System Managers, each with clearly defined access levels and permissions.

For Users

TechReserve is designed for FEU Tech students, faculty, and authorized personnel who need to reserve equipment or venues for academic purposes.

Users can submit a reservation by selecting a resource, completing the request form, and submitting it for administrative approval.

Reservation status updates are available in real time through the user dashboard.

Pending reservations may be modified or canceled by the user, while approved requests may require administrator intervention.

The system prevents conflicting bookings and encourages users to select alternative schedules or resources.
